Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at CalorieBuddy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos submitted through the bot for calorie analysis.
  • Basic metadata such as timestamps and device information.
  • Any optional information you choose to provide (e.g., feedback or inquiries).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze meal photos and provide calorie breakdowns.
  • To improve our services and enhance user experience.
  • To respond to user inquiries and feedback.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. Photos submitted for calorie analysis are deleted after processing.

6. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • You may opt out of data collection by discontinuing the use of our services.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our website or service.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@caloriebuddy.com.
